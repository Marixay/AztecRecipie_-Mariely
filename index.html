The Aztec Cake Recipie - By Mariely Cortes

After Baking

Historical Context

  1. This recipie is considered to be "The Mexican Lasagna"
  2. It's believed it was created sometime from 1300 to 1521
  3. The creators were "The Nahuas", from the Aztec Empire
  4. Its an honor to carry their heritage though my cooking

Ingredients

Tortillas 1 Kilo
Green Tomatoes 2 Kilos
Poblano peppers 5 Pieces
Corn niblets 150 gr
Serrano chillies 3 Pieces
Cilantro 1 Bunch
Onion 1 Piece
Garlic 1 Head
Panela Cheese 500 gr
Chicken breast, boiled and shreded 500 gr
Manchego (or mozzarela) cheese 300 gr
Salt As needed
Sour Cream 500 gr

Instructions

  1. Boil the chicken with salt, 1/4 of the onion and 2 cloves of garlic
  2. Once the checken has cool down, sheed all the chicken
  3. Boil the green tomatoes with the serrano chillies and 2 cloves of garlic.
  4. Once the tomatoes, chillies and garlic have cool down, put them in the blender with the leafs of the cilantro
  5. Heat the green sause for 20 minutes, till it boils
  6. Roast the poblano chillies in the fire
  7. Once they turn black put them in a plastic transparent bag so they "sweat"
  8. One by one, peal the poblano peppers
  9. Take the seed off and slice them in medium size stripes
  10. Fry the tortillas with a bit of oil in a pan, the consistency has to be a bit hard but not chrunchy
  11. Grab a pyrex container and start your first layer of the "Mexican Lasagna" put a layer of tortillas
  12. Cover your tortilla layer with the green sause
  13. Put 1/3 of the sheded chicken on top of the sauce
  14. Add the green pepperes stripes
  15. Add the panela cheese in bits
  16. Add the corn niblets
  17. Add sour cream and mozzarela cheese
  18. Start with another layer of tortilla and repeat steps 12 to 17, do this at least 3 times
  19. Pre heat the over to 350℉ (180℃) for 1 hour or until the upper layer looks golden brown
  20. Serve: Enjoy your delicious homemade Aztec Cake!! Your guests will always ask for a second serving!

Tips:

  • When you boil the green tomatoes, wait till they have slightly changed of colour
  • Do not boil the green tomatoes till their skin breakes, it will make your sauce bitter
  • Distribute the ingreedients in 3 parts to have enough for the 3 layers
  • Roast the green peppers from all sides so the peeling process is easier
  • When you do your sause, make sure the salt is adjusted when it boils
  • If your green pepers stripes are too spicy for you, you can boil them in salt for 10 minutes
